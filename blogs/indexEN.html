OUR QUALITY CHART

Our teams and the entire network are subject to a rigorous quality chart, guaranteeing the mastery of a proven know-how with a high-value clientele.

Quality is the keystone of our network, the only structured upscale network present in France. Any candidate for affiliation with us must respect strictly all the points notified in our quality chart. A good level of English and a certain number of years of experience are required to pretend to perform one of the transport missions entrusted by the central. Any driver must succeed professional tests to guarantee to the other co-affiliates that the skills required are well respected.

Quality controls during the missions are daily organized to ensure a consistent level of performance. The excellence has a cost and the first is to be able to question ourselves when defects are pointed by our quality operators.

he only objective pursued by the platform is to maintain a level of service faithful to our brand and to ensure that the affiliates in the position of contractors have all the guarantees of quality when they entrust their missions to the good care of their co-affiliates.

The better our affiliates, the better we can position ourselves, all together, in the major markets and events.

Quality is surely our key word.

OUR ECOLOGICAL CHART

SP Dev is committed for the environment.

Our network is strong of about 300 high-end vehicles of the latest generation to limit the carbon footprint.

Our actions :

Thanks to our software you’re able to inform your customers about the CO2 emissions for each mission.

Promoting the use of clean vehicles in our network and in our business : we give priority access to our network to companies using 100% electric vehicles, especially with high-end models (like Tesla S and Tesla X).

We encourage our affiliates to train and practice eco-driving daily.

Service Prestige has chosen for its own needs to use the latest generation hybrid vehicles.

SPD is taking anti-waste paper measures by promoting the exchange and storage of electronic data.
